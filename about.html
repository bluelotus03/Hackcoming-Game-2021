About

Game Story

The Holidays are almost here!

Some mischievous bunnies have taken all the presents and candy canes from the North Pole. To make matters worse, flying sun beams have broken through the ozone and threaten to melt all the ice!

How to Play

  • A
  • B
  • C

Use Santa's magic wand to reveal secret gifts and shoot snowballs!

You'll see boxes containing gifts [A], and you can use your magic with the ^ arrow to open the gift box and reveal the presents or candy canes. You can shoot snowballs with the S to destroy flying sunbeams and heal the melting ice [B] as you go. The more ice you restore to its solid winter state [C], the more points you can earn!

  • D
  • E
  • F
  • G

Jump around platforms to collect icicles and gifts!

When you use your magic on gift boxes, you will see a candy cane [D], an icicle [G], or one of the presents [E, F] appear on top of the box. You can jump with the space bar to reach platforms and get to items. The icicles will help you grow larger and jump higher, but you have to be quick! It will only last for a limited time. As santa, your job is to collect alll the gifts the bunnies stole. Each gift item you collect will earn you more points!

  • H
  • I

Avoid mischievous bunnies and flying sunbeams to reach the next level!

Bunnies [H] are ground-bound and will move left and right around the platforms, and sun beams [I] are air-bound and will fly up and down around the platforms. You can destroy both enemy types by jumping on them, but you can also destroy sun beams with your snowballs. Watch out for the sun beams zaps! They can kill you and melt the ice. Each enemy you destroy will earn you more points!

  • J

Approach lamps to go to the next level!

When you've collected all the gifts and destroyed all the enemies you can, head to the lamp post [J]! You will be transported to the next level and earn points for this accomplishment. Make sure you can get to the North Pole before the sun beams melt all the ice!

Version 2.0.0

Our second release has come with many bug fixes and some awesome new features! This time around, we have reverted our Kaboom JS version to a more stable release that allowed better use of collision functions and much more. After rewriting our game to use the older version of Kaboom JS, we were able to implement the following.

Added Functionality:

  • Improved and continuous enemy movement
  • Improved level design
  • Ability to freeze boxes to save the ice
  • Ability to destroy sun beams with snowballs
  • Limited casting rates for magic and snowballs
  • Sun beams fire zaps at the player

Bug Fixes from v1.0.0:

  • Object collision errors
  • Music looping into other scenes
  • Unlimited jumping
  • Magic and Snowball casting rates

Hackathons
